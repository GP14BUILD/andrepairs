Technology Meets Tradition

The GP14 is a two-person racing dinghy built in epoxy or wood, featuring spinnaker options and a rich international racing scene. Our hull design claimed first place at the 2022 World Championships in Skerries (Boat No. 14262).

Each boat is custom-built and spray-finished using FSC®-certified woods, premium epoxy, and top-quality fittings — balancing high performance with sustainability.

  • CAD and CFD-optimised hull
  • CNC-machined tooling
  • Spray-finished epoxy and varnish
  • FSC®-certified timber
  • Through-deck sheeting & curved coamings
  • Rotary-cut Sapelle hardwood decks
  • Custom fit-out configurations
  • CE and RCD compliant

GP14 GRP Foils

Our GRP foils are manufactured with precision-milled moulds for reliable, stiff and lightweight performance. Designed for consistency and speed, they’re ideal for serious racers and training fleets alike.
